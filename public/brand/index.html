08 — Usage

Do & don't

Keep the mark calm. The brand only earns trust by holding back.

Do

  • Use the mark on warm-white or deep-ink backgrounds only.
  • Keep clear-space around the mark equal to the height of the T-bar.
  • Reserve amber for small accents (verifications, dates, links).
  • Use Plus Jakarta Sans exclusively for headlines and copy.
  • Let the verification tick breathe — never on a busy ground.

Don't

  • Don't apply purple/blue gradient highlights to the mark.
  • Don't recolor the mark outside the approved palette.
  • Don't outline or stroke the wordmark.
  • Don't skew, rotate, or re-proportion individual letterforms.
  • Don't place the mark below the 24px minimum digital size.
